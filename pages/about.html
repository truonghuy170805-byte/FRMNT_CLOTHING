About us

We are not whole. And that's the point.
FRGMNT was built on the idea that nothing needs to
be perfect to be real. We don't follow a fixed form.
We break it, reshape it, and wear it differently. Every
piece you see is a fragment of identity, of emotion, of
chaos, of you.
