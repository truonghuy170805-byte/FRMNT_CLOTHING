STORY

FRGMNT believes that no one is made from a perfect mold. We are a collection of pieces from memories, culture, passions, and our own journeys. That's why FRGMNT was born to turn those pieces into a fashion language that's minimalist, strong, and full of personality. Every outfit isn't just meant to be worn, it's a way to tell your own story.

FRGMNT is built for those who embrace individuality. Through contemporary design and bold expression, the brand transforms personal identity into everyday style.

Born from the spirit of individuality, FRGMNT crafts fashion that reflects character, confidence, and a unique perspective on the world.

Wear your identity. Wear FRGMNT.
